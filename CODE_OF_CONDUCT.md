# Code of Conduct

Probe-Tech is committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive
community for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Assuming positive intent and collaborating in good faith
- Giving and receiving feedback gracefully

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Community maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments and contributions
that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
organization maintainers.
